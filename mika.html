    Chemical knowledge

    Mica chemical name is Hydrated aluminum potassium silicate, It's commonly formed by crystallization of magma(molten and semi-molten rock mixture from the surface of the earth) from hot gasses including vapors(pneuma), and hot solution. This process is called Muscovite it can also be found in granites, pegmatites, gneisses, and also schists.

    The chemical formula for mica is KAl2(AlSi3O10)(F,OH)2.
